My Kitchen

I don't normally cook, but my mom is a great cook and her cooking is what makes my kitchen a really good restaurant. She makes the best Vietnamese foods. Because of her, I never find the craving to eat Vietnamese food other than hers. This philosophy goes the same for any other food. I usually cook the foods that I can make better than restaurants. For example, if I want pancakes, I make them at home. I never find the need to eat pancakes outside because I already make them pretty decently at home and at a cheaper price.
